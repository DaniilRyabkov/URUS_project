URUS INTERIOR

The interior of the Urus Performante is designed for the driver and complemented by the use of innovative and lightweight materials

such as Alcantara and carbon fiber. These elements, dark colors, contrast stitching and Exclusive Performante logos are those unique additional details which emphasize the sporty character of the car. Refined, modern and ergonomic, The interior of the Urus Performante is designed in such a way that so that the driver “feels like a pilot.”

GENERAL OVERVIEW

A bold concept: Urus Performante is the sporty evolution of the first ever SSUV (Super Sport Utility Vehicle), defining a new market segment as the ultimate all-round supercar. With its distinctive sporty design, revolutionary use of lightweight components and improved aerodynamics, Urus Performante takes SUV driving pleasure and performance to a whole new level. This most exclusive model in the Urus family raises the bar for high-performance SUVs to new driving frontiers.
